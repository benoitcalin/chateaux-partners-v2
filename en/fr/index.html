An Encouter

Our story began with the encounter of two wine-making family from Saint-Émilion and Burgundy which gave birth in 2013 to our wine merchant company: Châteaux partners.

An Encouter

Our story began with the encounter of two wine-making family from Saint-Émilion and Burgundy which gave birth in 2013 to our wine merchant company: Châteaux partners.

From the beginning of our adventure, we have had the opportunity to offer our customers classified Grands Crus from Bordeaux, Saint-Émilion, the largest estates in Bourgogne and prestigious Maisons de Champagne.

Our Clients

We strive to satisfy our clientele, traditionnally composed of European and international companies, members of international business committees, government institutions, as well as self-made-(wo)men from Tokyo to New York city.

We share our passion for wine being attentive to individual needs and organising prestigious events all around the world, with a great concern for quality.
