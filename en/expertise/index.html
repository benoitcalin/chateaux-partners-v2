A Few Words

A rich portfolio for a wide range of offers.

Close links with our Châteaux and Maisons de Champagne to offer the best wines, ensuring quality and provenance of the products.

More than a wine merchant company, we consider ourselves as a wine club.

We share our passion for wine by organising monthly events.

Tastings

Every month we organise tastings in several cities in the world to discover and share the variety of our French wines and champagnes for an unparalleled experience.

Each of these wines and champagnes has a story to tell:

Each terroir, land and climate gives each grape variety its qualities and specificities.

Every family, man and woman working daily for these iconic Châteaux and Maisons has a passion to transmit.

Every Vintage and process of vinification from grape to bottle brings to the wines all its characteristics.

We are here to convey and reveal all the subtlety behind a bottle, as well as surprise and delight, we want to initiate our clientele fond of good wines and Champagnes.

Events

Our positioning allows us to travel and meet our clients. During these trips, we usually organize different kinds of events.

We are usually accompanied by representatives from each Chateau and Maisons de Champagne that will be tested.

We consider the transparency between our Châteaux and the final clientele as paramount. It is important for us to ensure the provenance of our wines and reassure our clientele about. It is also crucial that the representatives of Châteaux and Maisons meet our customers.

We are also eager to answer your needs regarding tastings, business dinners, tasting seminars or the preparation of all types of events (wedding, anniversary...).

Storage

Wine Tours

Thanks to our close relations with the professionals of the Bordeaux community, we have the opportunity to offer you wine tours.

You will be able to discover the vineyards of the Bordeaux region, with or without a driver, and taste wines during visits to the most prestigious châteaux.

A professional will prepare a program and will adapt to your preferences and advise you on the best places to go according to your interests.

In addition to this service, you can order from Châteaux Partners your favorite wines, delivered directly at home. This will prevent you from having to handle your wines personally if you are travelling by plane or train.

Do not hesitate to inquire about our offers, they are available throughout the year.
