Collaborators

Chateaux partners’ collaborators are united by our four values of humanism, openness, proximity and enthusiasm, which they bring every day to their work.

With a worldwide presence in more than 35 countries across the 5 continents; our employees, partners and clients represent a wide range of backgrounds, cultures and different lifestyles.

Viticulture

Agriculture is the basis of our activity, we work with more than 120 Châteaux and Maisons of Champagnes, including family exploitations. We accompany both reputed and aspiring Maisons and Châteaux in their commercial and human development.

We are in close collaboration with several winegrowers in Bordeaux, Cotes du Rhône and Cotes de Provence in the elaboration of our range "Envie de" and its different variations.

Our conception: to share what the earth can offer us.

Sustainable Development

We strive to promote partners caring about the environment and the functioning of society. Because we believe that viticulture should be part of an environmental package, we promote partners who use natural methods to exalt wine identity and preserve what the earth can offer.

Châteaux Partners, with its "Envie de" range, follows these guide-lines with a variety of sulphite-free wines.
